Tender Guidelines & Policies

Our Commitment to Ethical and Transparent Procurement

ADS–Nyanza upholds the highest standards of and fairness in all procurement activities. All processes are guided by Christian values, national procurement laws, and internal ethical policies to ensure equitable treatment of all vendors.

Prospective suppliers must be legally registered entities, compliant with all statutory and tax obligations, and possess demonstrable experience and capacity in their respective sectors. Documentation proving compliance is mandatory during submission.

All tender submissions must be received before the specified deadline outlined in each tender notice. Evaluation is conducted through a competitive, transparent, and criteria-based framework aligned with ADS–Nyanza’s procurement policy.

throughout the procurement lifecycle. All vendors are required to disclose any potential or actual conflicts of interest that may affect the integrity or impartiality of the procurement process.

ADS–Nyanza provides a structured mechanism for vendors to lodge appeals or complaints regarding procurement decisions. All disputes are handled impartially and in accordance with applicable laws and organizational policies to ensure fairness and accountability.
