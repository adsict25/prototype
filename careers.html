Recruitment Guidelines & Policies

Our Commitment to Fair & Transparent Hiring

ADS–Nyanza is an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity and inclusion. All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply regardless of gender, age, or background.

All applications must be submitted before the closing date as specified in the vacancy notice. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

ADS–Nyanza maintains confidentiality and professionalism throughout the recruitment process. Canvassing or influence peddling will lead to automatic disqualification.
